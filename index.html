Ethiopian food is a Washington D.C. staple. Come along with me as a grab food from my favorite Ethiopian carryout spot, Habesha Market.

Culture

Hassan II Mosque in Casablanca, Morocco is one of the world's largest Mosques in the world. The Mosque has a beautiful view of the cost and is open to visitors of all faiths.

Madrid has some of the best nightlife I've ever experienced. Tag along with my friends and I for a night out at Calle 365, one of my favorite bars with club energy.
